Our Unique Coffee Blends

Explore our curated selection of blends and single-origin coffees with tasting notes and brewing tips.

Morning Bliss

A smooth medium roast with notes of caramel and hazelnut. Perfect to start your day.

Tasting Notes: Caramel, Hazelnut, Mild Citrus

Recommended Brewing: Drip, French Press

Dark Velvet

A bold dark roast with rich chocolate and smoky undertones. Ideal for espresso lovers.

Tasting Notes: Dark Chocolate, Smoke, Bold

Recommended Brewing: Espresso, Moka Pot

Sunrise Single Origin

A light roast from Ethiopia with floral and citrus notes. Bright and refreshing.

Tasting Notes: Floral, Citrus, Bright

Recommended Brewing: Pour Over, Drip

Hazelnut Delight

A medium roast infused with natural hazelnut flavor. Smooth and nutty.

Tasting Notes: Nutty, Smooth, Sweet

Recommended Brewing: French Press, Drip

Moroccan Spice

A unique blend with cinnamon, cardamom, and clove notes. Warm and aromatic.

Tasting Notes: Cinnamon, Cardamom, Clove

Recommended Brewing: Espresso, Drip

Vanilla Cream

A smooth blend with natural vanilla flavor and creamy texture. Sweet and comforting.

Tasting Notes: Vanilla, Creamy, Sweet

Recommended Brewing: French Press, Pour Over
